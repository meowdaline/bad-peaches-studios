Why Bad?

Welcome to Bad Peaches Studios, an edgy and unconventional photography studio space located in Singapore. Our studio is not your typical photography studio - it's filled with tchotchkes, oddities, and other unique decor elements that add a distinctive character to every shoot.

We embrace the unconventional and seek to capture the beauty in the unusual, making our studio the perfect place for creatives who want to break free from the norm. Our space is equipped with cutting-edge lighting equipment, a variety of backdrops, and state-of-the-art cameras, providing photographers with everything they need to bring their visions to life.

Our team of experienced photographers and staff are here to help you navigate the unconventional and achieve your artistic goals. If you're looking for a photography studio space that's full of personality, Bad Peaches Studios is the perfect place for you.
